Beginner Yoga Course

Tue 18 Aug - Tue 22 Sep

Our 6-week courses are taught in a way that is encouraging for beginner students and will help you get started with a practice that can support you throughout your life.

Students start as beginners together and the course will develop confidence while starting a yoga practice. The beginner classes are progressive, and the course is designed to build a fundamental understanding of the method of Iyengar Yoga, and its use of props.
